Association of Women

Our Vision is to be the catalyst for the advancement of women in the engineering profession towards national and global technological development.

Our Work

Since 1982, APWEN has recorded a level of achievement of her objectives by advancing professional knowledge of members through:

Facilitation of continuing professional development workshops Organizing industrial visits to engineering facilities Career counselling sessions for female students in Primary, Secondary and Tertiary.
